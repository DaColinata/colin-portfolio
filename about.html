About the Artist

Based in Nashville, TN, I am a guitarist and composer specializing in the intersection of technical precision and emotive performance.

Currently completing my studies at Belmont University, my work ranges from complex session tracking to live performance with projects like The Evergreens. My approach is grounded in deep music theory—specifically Neo-Riemannian concepts—allowing me to navigate rock, funk, and jazz with a unique harmonic vocabulary.
